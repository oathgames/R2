# Example Brand

## Identity
- **Tagline**: Your Brand Tagline
- **URL**: https://yourbrand.com
- **Category**: Your Category (e.g., Streetwear, Skincare, Home Goods)

## Products
- **Product Name** ($XX): Brief description

## Brand Voice
- **Tone**: How your brand speaks (e.g., casual, professional, playful)
- **Audience**: Who you're targeting (age, interests, values)
- **Language style**: How copy should feel
- **Words to use**: on-brand vocabulary
- **Words to avoid**: off-brand vocabulary

## Visual Identity
- **Colors**: Your brand palette
- **Style**: Photo/ad aesthetic
- **Product details**: Key visual elements to always include

## UGC Guidelines
- Always mention: key selling points
- Never mention: competitors, things to avoid
- CTA style: how you ask people to buy
- Vibe: the feeling your content should evoke
